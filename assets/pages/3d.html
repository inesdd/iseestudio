ABSTRACT

In I SEE studio we think that what differentiates you from others is the way you think, the way you see things, and the ability you have to express it. Therefore, the following video talks about how to create a different space. Each project must be unique, therefore and following a certain trend of brands to promote their products within a surreal or abstract environment from geometric or organic shapes, this project called ABSTRACT was born, developed with the 3D modeling program, Blender.

TESYR

In the following video, we present the header that was carried out for a project of the creation of the complete visual identity for a research group in renewable energy called Tesyr. Like the previous one, this project was also carried out with the 3D modeling program, Blender.

SUBWAY

The project "Subway" consists of a short film, which shows us one of the everyday moments throughout any given day, also showing the appeal or charm that can have such a common action as waiting for your subway to pass while browsing through the different applications on your cell phone. This video also shows us the versatility of I SEE to face all kinds of disparate projects, whether they are more abstract or more realistic.
